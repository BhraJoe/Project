MADAME WEB – Official Trailer (HD)

Release Date: 2024

In a switch from the typical genre, Madame Web tells the
standalone origin story of one of Marvel publishing's most
enigmatic heroines. The suspense-driven thriller stars Dakota
Johnson as Cassandra Webb, a paramedic in Manhattan who
develops the power to see the future… and realizes she can use
that insight to change it. Forced to confront revelations about her
past, she forges a relationship with three young women bound
for powerful destinies...if they can all survive a deadly present.
